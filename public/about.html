Our Story

We started as a small kitchen table project in 2018. What began as a search for mindful moments turned into a mission: to bring thoughtfully sourced, small-batch teas and rituals to people who want more presence in their day.

Our Values

  • Regenerative sourcing
  • Transparent partnerships
  • Mindful rituals over mindless consumption

We visit our partner farms each season, tasting with producers and building multi-year relationships that ensure fair pay and environmental care.

Sustainability

We use compostable packaging when possible, minimize single-use plastics, and offset shipment emissions for orders over a threshold. Transparency is paramount — every batch page includes farm details and harvest notes.
